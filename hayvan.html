T I G E R

The tiger, scientifically known as Panthera tigris, is a majestic and powerful big cat that captivates the imagination with its distinctive orange coat adorned with dark stripes.
As the largest member of the Felidae family, the tiger possesses a robust build and muscular frame, reflecting its prowess as a formidable predator.
With a keen sense of sight and exceptional agility, tigers are known for their stealth and prowess in hunting.
These solitary creatures are found in various habitats, from dense forests to grasslands, across Asia.
Unfortunately, several subspecies of tigers are endangered due to habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflicts.
Efforts in conservation and awareness are crucial to ensuring the survival of these magnificent creatures, as they continue to symbolize both the beauty and the vulnerability of the natural world.
